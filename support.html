Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my purchases?

Open the app → Settings → Restore Purchases. Make sure you're signed in to the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

I completed a level but my stars weren't saved. What happened?

Progress is saved automatically at the end of each level. If it didn't save, try completing the level again. If the problem persists, contact us.

The ball got stuck or the game froze. What should I do?

Force-close the app and reopen it. Your progress up to the start of that level will be preserved.

How do I connect to Game Center?

Game Center sign-in is handled automatically when you launch the app. Make sure you're signed in to Game Center in your iPhone's Settings app.

Can I play on multiple devices with the same progress?

Game Center leaderboards and achievements sync across devices. Level progress is stored locally on each device.

I was charged but didn't receive my purchase.

Go to Settings → Restore Purchases. If that doesn't resolve it, contact us with your Apple Order ID and we'll look into it.
