Privacy Policy

Paddle Maniac  ·  Last updated: March 26, 2026

Paddle Maniac ("the app") is developed by Gigglebytelabs. This policy explains what data the app does and does not collect.

Data We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The app does not require you to create an account.

Data Stored on Your Device

The app saves the following data locally on your device only, using Apple's standard storage (UserDefaults and the app's Documents folder):

  • Game progress (levels completed, stars earned)
  • High scores
  • Settings preferences (sound, haptics, graphics)
  • In-app purchase status

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

Apple Game Center

If you choose to sign in to Game Center, your scores and achievements are submitted to Apple's Game Center service. This is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. We do not have access to your Game Center profile or data.

In-App Purchases

Purchases are processed entirely by Apple. We do not receive or store any payment information. Apple's handling of purchase data is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Advertising

The app contains no advertising.

Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised date.
